About US

The initiative was started in March 2018 with a team of 8 working professionals and 8 students. The aim was simply to help and educate the underprivileged. Primarily, our first teaching centre was in Sector 14, Gurgoan. After many efforts we got a positive response from the children’s parents. The parents after conferring with their children and assessing their results quoted:

“If we had not joined Alankaar a few months back, passing in the final exam itself would have been difficult.”

The above line was the only source of motivation while pushing through the lack of time, volunteers and resources. Through that ray of hope, volunteers slowly began to show up and with resonance to that we were able to expand; we opened two new centeres in Gurgaon (Sector 15 & 45). Not only were the children passing their final exams but many were scoring above 90%. The whole perspective of our team took a paradigm shift when the focus extended to the overall development of the children. Thus, came various events, activities and festive celebrations.

Now, with all efforts struck in, we are successfully running the NGO with 150+ students and 100 odd volunteers. Alankaar immensely appreciates the society for the donation of time to help and educate our tender budding minds. We not only got contributions from our locals, but the support has been worldwide. These contributions from numerous people further helped us in necessary resources to the underprivileged children.
